About Us

Who We Are

Welcome to Autism Info, a platform dedicated to providing accurate and accessible information about autism. We started this journey on with a mission to raise awareness and support for individuals with autism and their families.

Our Motivation

Our primary motivation for creating this website is to increase awareness about autism and make reliable information easily accessible to everyone. We aim to create a safe, supportive space where families, caregivers, and individuals can find resources, guidance, and a sense of community.

Our Mission

We believe that knowledge empowers individuals and families. By providing comprehensive information on autism, early intervention, and support, we hope to enhance the quality of life for people with autism and their loved ones. Together, we can make a difference!
